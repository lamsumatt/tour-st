  • OUR COMMUNITY

    • Whether you have never visited St. Patrick’s Cathedral or if you are an active parishioner, we invite you to get more involved in our community. 


      There are several ways to get involved: 

      • Become a Parishioner – Midtown Manhattan is one of the busiest, most exciting areas in the world, the bustling businessmen and shoppers only interrupted by the calm serenity of St. Patrick’s Cathedral. Become a parishioner and join our community of faith.

      • Sign Our Guestbook – We’ll keep you “in the loop” with regular email communications throughout the year so that you’ll be among the first to know about services, special events, concerts and more. Whether you are a frequent or occasional visitor of the Cathedral, being a member of the Guestbook will allow you to always stay in the know with all the exciting new things happening at St. Patrick’s Cathedral.

      • Share your St. Patrick’s Story – More than five million people visit St Patrick’s every year and each visit comes with its own unique story. We would love to hear your favorite memory or story about our beloved Cathedral. Click here to read what some others have shared recently.

      • Support The Cathedral – Since the cornerstone to St. Patrick's Cathedral was laid in 1858, generous donors have allowed the Cathedral to remain a beacon of faith on Fifth Avenue. Support the Cathedral and become part of the global community that preserves one of America's most iconic structures.

      • Volunteer – The Cathedral is home to over five million visitors each and every year from all corners of the world. Please consider reviewing the many different opportunities there are to volunteer, and you can join the community of volunteers that enhance the experience of St. Patrick’s Cathedral for all who walk through our doors.
